This is BombSquad Explodinary. 
It is a modpack which enhances what's best in vanilla, while also adding it's own quirks and tweaks. 
It's purpose is to give players a compromise between unmodified and heavily modded builds of BombSquad. 
Bombs Away!

If you don't own BombSquad Pro and therefore can't make your own playlists - don't worry!

FFA - 420618
Teams - 420619

To import playlists, simply click "Customize" in FFA or Teams play tab.
Then click "Import", and type the codes I provided.
